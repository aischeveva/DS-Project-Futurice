Industry trends based on the annual reports

For a consulting company like Futurice, it is important to understand emerging business trends and the market situation of client companies.

The task of this project:
  • Explore publicly available textual financial datasets using machine learning and data analysis methods
  • Create visualizations to highlight the most important trends in the data
  • Is it possible to extract market trends automatically from the published data?

    • Data Source:
  • Edgar database
  • For the analysis we were using 10K reports

    • Navigation
  • You can find timeseries charts and wordclouds for topics for each industry in the respective tab from the dashboard
  • Industries are grouped by Offices
